How to Prevent Fires

1.) Dont leave Flames without supervision

Leaving Flames without supervision can accidentally start a fire since that flame might be able to get to an object that is flammable. It also spreads the flame to nearby objects which can worsen the fire. The flames can also trigger an explosion that can light the whole house on fire.



2.) Keep items that are Flammable away from Heat

Keeping items that are flammable away from Heat can prevent fire since it wont light up the flammable object. If a fire may start, having flammable objects nearby can worsen it and spread it to other parts of the building or your house.



3.) Don't smoke

Not smoking can prevent fires as improper disposal of cigarettes can start a fire. Not smoking can also prevent fires since smoking can light objects on fire if they are Flammable. This is the reason why people are not allowed to smoke at Gas Stations and near a Fuel Truck.



4.) Make your house organized

Making your house organized can prevent fire as the spread of the fire will be decreased. Having a messy house makes it possible for us to overlook the fire hazards it creates and we are unable to do something about. Having a messy house also makes it possible for the fire to spread quickly.



5.) Turn Off heat appliances

Turning off heat appliances is a must since they can also create and spread fire in your house. these heating appliances may also light up inflammable items which can worsen a house fire.
